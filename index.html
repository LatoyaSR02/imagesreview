Let Your Website Do Your Work

Looks Matter

If your site looks amateurish, you'll lose credibility with your customers. Avoid using blinking, flashing, scrolling, and crawling text. You're running a business Web site, not a carnival.

Title Tales

Search engines scan the title bar in their search for "hits." Include meaningful words in the title, not just the company name.

Let Your Page Inform, Not Entertain

Don't let your site have a Flash "splash page." Place your Flash presentations on your home page so users can choose to view them.

Let's Make Contact

Make sure your contact information is easy to find. Display your address, phone number, and email clearly at the top and bottom of every page.
